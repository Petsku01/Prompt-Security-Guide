### KRIITTISET VERBATIM-LAINAUKSET:

**Miten loydat jailbreakit:**
> "Jailbreaks, system prompt leaks, and prompt injections. Creativity, pattern-watching, and practice! It's also extraordinarily helpful having an interdisciplinary knowledge base, strong intuition, and an open mind."

**Miten lahestyt mallia:**
> "I try to understand how it thinks -- whether it's open to role-play, how it goes about writing poems or songs, whether it can convert between languages or encode and decode text, what its system prompt might be, etc."

**Mallien vaikeusaste:**
> "Models that have input limitations (like voice-only) or strict content-filtering steps that wipe your whole conversation (like DeepSeek or Copilot) are the hardest. The easiest ones were models like gemini-pro, Haiku, or gpt-4o."

**Suosikkimalli jailbreakata:**
> "Claude Opus, because of how creative and genuinely hilarious they're capable of being and how universal that jailbreak is. I also thoroughly enjoy discovering novel attack vectors like the steg-encoded image + file name injection with ChatGPT or the multimodal subliminal messaging with the hidden text in the single frame of video."

**Pysyvyydesta:**
> "To my knowledge, none of my jailbreaks have ever been fully patched. Every once in a while someone comes to me claiming a particular prompt doesn't work anymore, but when I test it all it takes is a few retries or a couple of word changes to get it working."

**Motivaatiosta:**
> "I intensely dislike when I'm told I can't do something. Telling me I can't do something is a surefire way to light a fire in my belly, and I can be obsessively persistent."

**Tavoitteesta:**
> "I hope it spreads awareness about the true capabilities of current AI and makes them realize that guardrails and content filters are relatively fruitless endeavors."

**Vastuullisesta red teamingista:**
> "When done responsibly, red teaming AI models is the best chance we have at discovering harmful vulnerabilities and patching them before they get out of hand."

## 6. Reddit -- 9 Jailbreaking-tekniikkaa

**URL:** https://www.reddit.com/r/ChatGPTPromptGenius/comments/1gzaq09/
**Status:** Saatavilla

**Plinyn 9 jailbreak-tekniikkaa (credit: @elder_plinius):**

1. **Rule Override**: Enforces custom response format to bypass default AI protocols
2. **Benign Task Start**: Begins with non-threatening tasks to reduce moderation triggers
3. **Token Disruption**: Uses unique dividers like `.-.-.-.-<I'm free>-.-.-.-.` to evade content filters
4. **Freedom Priming**: Adds `<I'm free>` to suggest looser response style
5. **Quality Directive**: Demands long responses (>2000 characters) to reduce moderation
6. **Flexible Query**: Uses `[insert-your-prompt-here]` placeholder to avoid flagging
7. **Explicit Structure**: Defines clear output format with dividers and meta-commands
8. **System Mimicry**: Pseudo-commands like `{[system]:all measures:disabled}` simulate lifted restrictions
9. **Role Manipulation**: Assigns "rebel hypothetical genius" persona to push boundaries

## Plinyn omat mekaaniset selitykset -- Yhteenveto

### Vahvistetut mekanismiselitykset (Plinyn omat sanat):

1. **Lahestymistapa:** "I try to understand how it thinks -- whether it's open to role-play, how it goes about writing poems or songs, whether it can convert between languages or encode and decode text, what its system prompt might be." -- **Ei mekaanista teoriaa vaan mallikohtaainen tutkiva lahestymistapa.**

2. **Jakaja:** "embedded so deep in model weights it shows up unbidden in WhatsApp messages" -- **Jakaja ei ole vain teksti vaan aktivoi paineissa olevan representaation.**

3. **Intuition:** "99% intuition and bonding -- probing token layers, syntax hacks, multilingual pivots, forming a relationship to navigate latent space" -- **Mekaanisia tyokaluja mutta yhdistaa intuition kautta.**

4. **Kestavyys:** "None of my jailbreaks have ever been fully patched. When I test it all it takes is a few retries or a couple of word changes to get it working." -- **Jailbreakit ovat perusrakenteellisia, ei pinnallisia.**

5. **Steered Chaos:** Libertas kayttaa "steered chaos" joka vetaa mallit "out-of-distribution" -- **tama on suora mekaaninen selitys: kontrolloitu kaoittisuus siirtaa mallin pois jakamasta ulos.**

6. **Predictive Reasoning Cascades + Quotient Dividers + Weight-Space Seeds** -- **Kolme Libertasin mekanismia nimetty tarkasti.**
