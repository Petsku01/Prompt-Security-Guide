## Key Themes Across Sources

1. **Indirect Prompt Injection (IPI)** is the #1 concern in 2026 — Google's threat intel confirms it's moving from theoretical to actively exploited.
2. **Multimodal attack surfaces** (image-based injection) are the emerging frontier — text-only defenses are insufficient.
3. **OWASP LLM Top 10 (2025)** retains prompt injection at #1 — this remains the industry-standard risk classification.
4. **Agentic systems** introduce new vulnerability classes — RAG pipeline poisoning, MCP security, tool-use exploitation.
5. **Regulatory alignment** is now required — NIST, EU AI Act, ISO 42001 all have prompt security implications.
6. **Production deployment gap** — ~70% of RAG pipelines tested are vulnerable to trivial attacks, indicating a severe gap between research and deployment.
7. **Defense comparatives are emerging** — TokenMix's ranking of 8 tested techniques and CSA's defense analysis represent a shift toward empirical defense evaluation.
