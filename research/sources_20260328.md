## AI Safety Alignment Research

**Title:** What Matters For Safety Alignment?  
**URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03868  
**Date:** January 7, 2026  

**Summary:**
Comprehensive empirical study on safety alignment capabilities across 32 LLMs and LRMs (3B-235B parameters). Evaluates influence of model characteristics and attack techniques through 4.6M API calls testing 56 jailbreak techniques. Key finding: models with integrated reasoning and self-reflection show superior safety alignment.

**Why Relevant:**
Recent academic research providing empirical data on what makes models more resistant to safety attacks. Valuable for understanding defensive mechanisms and model selection for secure applications.
