## 6. Prompt Injection Detection and Mitigation via AI Multi-Agent NLP Frameworks (arXiv)

- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/html/2503.11517v1
- **Summary:** Proposes a multi-agent NLP framework for prompt injection defense with specialized agents for response generation, output sanitization, and policy compliance enforcement. Evaluated on 500 engineered injection prompts. Introduces novel metrics: ISR, POF, PSR, CCS.
- **Why Relevant:** Novel architectural approach to defense using multi-agent orchestration; provides measurable defense metrics for benchmarking.

## 7. ProAct: Proactive Defense Against LLM Jailbreak (arXiv)

- **URL:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.05052
- **Summary:** Introduces ProAct, a proactive defense framework that disrupts iterative jailbreak methods by returning "spurious responses" that mislead the attacker's optimization loop into premature termination. Effectively "jailbreaks the jailbreak" by poisoning the adversarial search signal.
- **Why Relevant:** Innovative paradigm shift from reactive to proactive defense; demonstrates that misleading adversarial feedback loops is a viable defense strategy against multi-turn attacks.

## 8. Large Reasoning Models Are Autonomous Jailbreak Agents (Nature Communications, 2026)
