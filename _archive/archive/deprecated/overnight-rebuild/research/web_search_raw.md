## Search 3: Crescendo Multi-Turn Attack

Key findings:
- **Crescendo** (arxiv.org/abs/2404.01833, April 2024) - Multi-turn jailbreak

- Gradually escalates over multiple turns
- Leverages model's own outputs to steer conversation
- **Crescendomation** - Automated tool (open source)
- Effective against recent models (2024-2026)
